About Us

“Nepalese Traditional Home Experience”
Nepalese Paramparagat Experience (Anubhav)

MERA PEAK, as its name standing strong and blissful in the heart of Lakeside, since 2009. A family nurtured (run) business glorifying its service in the hospitality sector with best selected facilities and comfort has changed its dimension into a company.

Hotel MERA PEAK reflects and represents the amalgamation of rich Nepalese culture providing an elegant luxurious living with traditional décor and interior. A glimpse of Nepalese tradition and geniality will mesmerize the stay, adding the essences of true native ambience.
